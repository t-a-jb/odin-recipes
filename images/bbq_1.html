Gourmet BBQ Catering in Melbourne


Bring Five-Star luxury into your gourmet catering with our polished stainless steel Deluxe rolltop chafers.

The ultimate BBQ fusion, our Gourmet BBQ catering is known for its delicious food, modern style and competitive price. We cater for large or small gatherings, family and work celebrations.

We’ve got the BBQ catering needs covered with our selection of succulent meats, expertly prepared by our experienced chefs. We fire up our grills on the day, and go to great lengths to produce a range of incredible, fresh meals for your event.

Our entire gourmet BBQ catering meal options are entirely customisable, and we can even add in particular home favourites for you, making your event one to remember.

The proof is in the preparation!

As the premier provider of catering service, we go to great lengths to ensure our food is prepared perfectly every time. Your carefully prepared BBQ catering selection is made with the finest herbs, spices and marinades. We also accommodate a wide range of different cuisines, from the spice of Iberia, to the unique flavours of the Orient!

Our caterers can stay for the duration of the event, to ensure the ease of serving of your barbecue platter. They can even cook on-site if needed for the true BBQ experience!

Tailoring our services to your dietary needs

As the city's premier catering service, we understand the importance of certain dietary requirements. Our entire range of BBQ catering options can be customised, ensuring that everyone can enjoy the tasty food that we offer! We have a wide selection of excellent vegetarian options, which are equally delicious. We can even cater for those who require a gluten free diet effortlessly.

We are also open to adding to our selection, should there be a particular favourite that everyone is looking forward to. Feel free to advise our caterers on any particular requirements that you may have!

Gourmet barbecue buffets!

Everyone loves a buffet, and your guests, co-workers or colleagues will simply love our grill buffets. Our chefs will be on hand to deliver the finest collection of buffet meat dishes - both hot and cold - as well as ingredients which will make your occasion unforgettable.

On top of our BBQ selection, we also have a great variety of dessert options, perfect for closing off the event on a sweet note!
